Our Story

A Warm Welcome to
Mas del Rey

Located on the sun-drenched Costa del Azahar, just eight kilometers from Benicarló and Vinaròs, our boutique B&B is a secluded haven surprisingly close to the Mediterranean sea.

We are Alijda and Bastien de Jong. We shared a common dream for a long time: to create a place where hospitality, tranquility, and a sense of belonging converge. In 2025, that dream became a reality when we fell in love with this beautiful, verdant region of authentic Spain.

Whether you seek a quiet retreat for two, a gathering place for friends, or an inspiring location for retreats, we ensure a relaxed, informal atmosphere combined with absolute privacy and that little bit of extra attention.
